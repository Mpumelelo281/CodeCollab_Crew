General Questions

CodeCollab Crew is a collaboration platform designed to connect students with faculty members for academic project collaboration. It enables lecturers to create projects and students to apply, join teams, and contribute to meaningful work.

CodeCollab Crew is available to students and faculty members with valid institutional email addresses (ending with .co.za). Both students and lecturers can register and participate in projects.

Yes, CodeCollab Crew is completely free for all registered students and faculty members of participating institutions.

For Students

Browse available projects, find one that matches your interests and skills, and click "Apply to Join". You'll need to submit an application which will be reviewed by the project owner.

Yes, you can apply to and participate in multiple projects simultaneously, as long as you can manage your time and commitments effectively.

All your contributions are logged on the platform. Project owners can see your submissions, and you can build a portfolio of your work over time.

For Faculty

Once logged in, navigate to "Create Project" from the dashboard. Fill in the project details, requirements, milestones, and publish when ready.

Yes, you can specify required skills, tools, and prerequisites when creating your project. You can also review each application individually before accepting.
